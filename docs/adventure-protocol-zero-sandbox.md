# Protocol Zero: The Neo-Seoul Sandbox

**A Megadungeon & Sandbox Campaign for Levels 1–10**
**Setting:** Neo-Seoul, Modern Urban Fantasy
**System:** System Ascendant (d20 / 5e-Compatible)

> *"The System isn't a game. It's an ecosystem, and we are the food."*

## Introduction
The Protocol Zero campaign has been expanded into a fully realized Sandbox. Drawing structural inspiration from *Curse of Strahd*, this campaign focuses on exploration, dynamic faction politics, and massive procedurally generated threats. The characters are free to explore Neo-Seoul's districts, conquer random Gates, and align with factions before the inevitable S-Rank Gate Break.

---

### Abyssal Metropolis
**Type:** City | **Threat Level:** B-Rank

A sprawling city built within the caldera of an active volcano, where demons and mortals once coexisted in an uneasy alliance. The Abyssal Metropolis is now a ruin of obsidian streets and magma-flooded districts, home to B-rank ascendants seeking demonic artifacts. The city's central spire contains the throne room where the Demon King once held court, and many claim his essence still lingers, tempting visitors with promises of power.

## Procedural Appendix A: Comprehensive Relics List

This massive arsenal represents the loot available throughout the sandbox. Items can be seeded into Gate boss chests or Black Market shops.

#### Lightning Blade
*Weapon, legendary — Requires Attunement*
A rapier forged from a solidified lightning strike, vibrating with ultra-high frequency mana.

**Abilities:**
- ***Lightning Strike.*** The sword deals extra lightning damage and can chain to nearby enemies.
- ***Thunder Clap.*** Once per turn, can create a thunderous boom that deafens enemies within 10 feet.
- ***Storm Fury.*** Once per day, call down a lightning storm that strikes all enemies in 30-foot radius.

#### Bloodthirsty Greatsword
*Weapon, very_rare — Requires Attunement*
A jagged black slab of iron that hums with the rhythm of a heartbeat. It does not just cut; it drinks.

**Abilities:**
- ***Blood Frenzy.*** After reducing a creature to 0 HP, gain advantage on attack rolls and +2 damage for 1 minute.
- ***Life Drain.*** Once per turn when you hit a creature, you can deal extra necrotic damage equal to your Strength modifier.
- ***Blood Healing.*** When you kill a creature, regain HP equal to half the damage you dealt.

#### Abyssal Plate Armor
*Armor, legendary — Requires Attunement*
Armor forged in the deepest shadows, offering protection while enhancing shadow abilities.

**Abilities:**
- ***Shadow Meld.*** As a bonus action, become incorporeal for 1 minute, moving through walls and creatures.
- ***Darkness Aura.*** Magical darkness surrounds you in a 15-foot radius. You can see through this darkness.
- ***Shadow Fortification.*** When you would take damage, you can use your reaction to reduce it by half.

#### Regent's Shadow Dagger
*Weapon, legendary — Requires Attunement*
A jagged shard of the First Singularity, wreathed in flickering black mana. It does not cut flesh; it deletes the spatial coordinates of whatever it touches.

**Abilities:**
- ***Shadow Pierce.*** The dagger ignores all armor and resistance, dealing damage directly to the target's HP.
- ***Dimensional Strike.*** As a bonus action, teleport up to 60 feet and make a dagger attack against one creature.
- ***Essence Drain.*** On a critical hit, drain the target's life essence, gaining temporary HP equal to the damage dealt.
- ***Shadow Portal.*** Once per day, create a portal between two points you can see within 120 feet.

#### Skywyrm's Gauntlet
*Armor, mythic — Requires Attunement*
The legendary gauntlet of the Umbral Regent, containing the power to command shadows and bend reality.

**Abilities:**
- ***Shadow Command.*** As an action, take control of all shadow creatures within 120 feet for 1 minute.
- ***Reality Bend.*** Once per week, alter reality in a 30-foot radius for 1 minute.
- ***Regent's Protection.*** You have resistance to all damage types and advantage on all Decree checks.
- ***Umbral Legion.*** Summon an army of 100 Umbral Legion that serve you for 24 hours.

#### Shadow Elixir
*Consumable, very_rare*
A dark elixir that grants temporary shadow abilities.

**Abilities:**
- ***Shadow Empowerment.*** Drink to gain shadow step and shadow stealth for 1 hour.

#### Shadow Lens
*Tool, rare*
A protocol-enhanced lens that can see through shadows and reveal hidden things.

**Abilities:**
- ***Shadow Vision.*** Can see through shadows and protocol-enhanced darkness.
- ***True Sight.*** Can see through illusions and detect invisible creatures.
- ***Shadow Detection.*** Can detect shadow creatures and shadow protocol.

#### Essence Collector
*Tool, very_rare — Requires Attunement*
A device that can collect and store life essence from defeated enemies.

**Abilities:**
- ***Essence Collection.*** Automatically collects essence from defeated enemies within 30 feet.
- ***Essence Storage.*** Can store up to 20 essence points.
- ***Essence Release.*** Can release stored essence to heal or power abilities.

#### Frost Axe
*Weapon, very_rare — Requires Attunement*
An axe carved from permafrost that radiates a field of absolute zero.

**Abilities:**
- ***Ice Touch.*** The axe deals extra cold damage and can freeze enemies solid.
- ***Winter's Grasp.*** Once per day, can create a blizzard in 60-foot radius for 1 minute.
- ***Frost Shield.*** Can create a wall of ice that blocks attacks and movement.

#### Dragon Blood Potion
*Consumable, legendary*
A potion made from dragon blood that grants draconic abilities.

**Abilities:**
- ***Draconic Power.*** Drink to gain dragon breath weapon and resistance for 1 hour.

#### Essence Potion
*Consumable, rare*
A potion containing concentrated life essence that can heal wounds and enhance abilities.

**Abilities:**
- ***Essence Restoration.*** Drink to restore 4d4+4 HP and gain +1 to all ability checks for 1 hour.

#### Dragon Scale Mail
*Armor, legendary — Requires Attunement*
Armor crafted from the scales of an ancient dragon, offering exceptional protection and dragon-like abilities.

**Abilities:**
- ***Dragon Resistance.*** Resistance to the damage type of the dragon whose scales were used.
- ***Dragon Breath.*** Once per day, breathe a cone of dragon energy.
- ***Dragon Frightful Presence.*** Creatures within 30 feet must make Sense check or be frightened.

#### Architect's Authority
*Accessory, legendary — Requires Attunement*
An artifact that grants the bearer command over lesser shadows and the respect of all beings.

**Abilities:**
- ***Command Shadows.*** As an action, issue a command to all shadow creatures within 60 feet. They must obey if they fail a Sense check.
- ***Royal Presence.*** All creatures except those with higher CR have disadvantage on attack rolls against you.
- ***Inspire Loyalty.*** Your allies within 30 feet gain +2 to Decree checks and cannot be frightened.
- ***Decree.*** Once per week, issue a decree that affects all creatures in a 1-mile radius.

#### Dimensional Compass
*Accessory, very_rare*
A compass that points toward dimensional disturbances and can guide through abyssal realms.

**Abilities:**
- ***Dimension Sense.*** Always points toward the nearest dimensional disturbance within 100 miles.
- ***Shadow Navigation.*** Cannot get lost in abyssal realms or dimensional spaces.
- ***Portal Detection.*** Vibrates when within 100 feet of a portal or dimensional rift.
- ***Wayfinding.*** Once per day, can show the safest path through dangerous dimensional areas.

#### Bloodstone Amulet
*Accessory, epic — Requires Attunement*
An amulet that enhances shadow manipulation abilities and protects against shadow corruption.

**Abilities:**
- ***Shadow Enhancement.*** Your shadow abilities deal +1d6 extra damage and have advantage on attack rolls.
- ***Blood Protection.*** Immunity to shadow corruption and advantage on saves against shadow spells.
- ***Essence Storage.*** Can store up to 10 essence points that can be used to power shadow abilities.
- ***Shadow Sight.*** Can see in protocol-enhanced darkness and perceive invisible creatures.

#### Crown of Sense
*Accessory, legendary — Requires Attunement*
A crown that grants the wearer immense knowledge and insight.

**Abilities:**
- ***All-Knowledge.*** You have proficiency in all skills and can add double proficiency bonus to Intelligence checks.
- ***True Sight.*** Can see through illusions and perceive the true nature of things.
- ***Sense of Ages.*** Once per day, can gain insight into any question by consulting the crown.
- ***Mental Fortress.*** Immunity to psychic damage and advantage on all mental Decree checks.

#### Shadow Orb
*Accessory, epic — Requires Attunement*
A sphere of pure shadow energy that can be used to power shadow abilities.

**Abilities:**
- ***Shadow Power.*** The orb contains 10 shadow charges that can be used to power shadow abilities.
- ***Shadow Shield.*** As a reaction, expend a charge to create a shield that blocks one attack.
- ***Shadow Bolt.*** As an action, expend a charge to fire a bolt of shadow energy.
- ***Shadow Renewal.*** The orb regains 1d4 charges at dawn.

#### Time Turner
*Accessory, mythic — Requires Attunement*
A device that can manipulate time in limited ways.

**Abilities:**
- ***Time Rewind.*** Once per week, can rewind time by 6 seconds, undoing recent events.
- ***Precognition.*** Advantage on all attack rolls and Decree checks.
- ***Haste.*** Once per day, can take an extra turn.
- ***Slow Time.*** Once per day, can force all enemies in 30-foot radius to act in slow motion.

## Appendix G: Pacing & Advancement

**Decree Wardens have two options for pacing this sandbox campaign:**

1. **Milestone Leveling (Narrative Focus):** Characters level up by achieving major story beats (e.g., closing a Red Gate, stopping a Null Guild ritual, reaching a faction capstone).
2. **Essence Harvesting (XP Focus):** Characters absorb Essence from the Anomalies they slay in the Sandbox. Every D-Rank gate yields 1,000 XP equivalent. To level, characters must return to a Safehouse and "process" their essence.
