# Legal-Safe Audio Sources for DMs

This guide helps you find and use legal-safe audio for your D&D campaigns.

## Important Legal Notes
- Always check the license for each track
- Keep track of attribution requirements
- Commercial use rules may vary by source
- When in doubt, choose CC0 (Public Domain) content

## Recommended Audio Sources

### YouTube Audio Library
**URL:** https://studio.youtube.com/audio_library
**License:** Free to use, no attribution required
**Description:** Free music and sound effects for YouTube videos

**Features:**
- Thousands of tracks
- No copyright issues
- Safe for commercial use
- High quality audio
- Search and filter options

**How to Use:**
1. Visit youtube.com/audio_library
2. Search for music or sound effects
3. Download your chosen tracks
4. Upload to Solo Compendium

**Categories:** Music, Sound Effects

---

### FreeSound
**URL:** https://freesound.org
**License:** Various CC licenses (check each track)
**Description:** Collaborative database of CC-licensed sounds

**Features:**
- Huge sound effects library
- User-contributed content
- Advanced search filters
- Community ratings
- Download in multiple formats

**How to Use:**
1. Create a free account
2. Search for sounds
3. Filter by CC0 license for no attribution
4. Download and credit if required

**Categories:** Sound Effects, Ambient, Voices

---

### OpenGameArt.org
**URL:** https://opengameart.org
**License:** Various CC and custom licenses
**Description:** Open source game assets including audio

**Features:**
- Game-focused audio
- Music and sound effects
- Loopable tracks
- Community curated
- Free for commercial use (check license)

**How to Use:**
1. Browse audio section
2. Check license for each track
3. Download if suitable
4. Attribute if required

**Categories:** Music, Sound Effects, Ambient

---

### Zapsplat
**URL:** https://www.zapsplat.com
**License:** Free with attribution option
**Description:** Free sound effects with attribution option

**Features:**
- Professional sound effects
- High quality recordings
- Attribution-free option (paid)
- Regular free updates
- Search and categorization

**How to Use:**
1. Sign up for free account
2. Browse sound effects
3. Download with required attribution
4. Upgrade for attribution-free

**Categories:** Sound Effects

---

### Incompetech
**URL:** https://incompetech.com
**License:** Creative Commons (attribution required)
**Description:** Royalty-free music by Kevin MacLeod

**Features:**
- High quality compositions
- Various genres and moods
- Consistent style
- Widely used in media
- Free for commercial use

**How to Use:**
1. Browse music collection
2. Choose appropriate tracks
3. Download in desired format
4. Provide attribution in credits

**Categories:** Music

## Quick Start Guide

1. **Start with YouTube Audio Library** - Easiest to use, no attribution needed
2. **Add FreeSound for effects** - Great for ambient sounds and effects
3. **Use Incompetech for music** - High quality, just need attribution
4. **Check licenses carefully** - Always verify before using
5. **Keep attribution records** - Note where you got each track
